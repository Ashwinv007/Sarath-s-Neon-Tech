Sarath.S is a Youtuber and tech influencer. He owns three channels. The channels cover motifs related to Technology, Science, Tips, Tricks, Software, Apps,Etc. Occasional Vlogs also appear.

 

He entered the world of youtube due to his passion towards video editing, Teaching & Technology. The major tech influencers were an inspiration for him. His first video of WhatsApp tricks got viral, which motivated him to upload further videos.

 

He works as an Educator in the Global Education Center of Infosys by giving corporate training to B.tech, M.tech, BCA, MCA and BSE students.

 

In his childhood, Sarath wanted to be a singer, but due to some incidents, it was not continued. He completed his higher secondary in another school. His classmates used to mock him by calling him "Neon" since Sarath's character was like the element Neon's properties. After so many insults, he decided to start his channel with the name of Neon and his plan was to grow so that those who mocked him earlier be worried.

 

He completed his College in SCMS School of Engineering & Technology as an Electronics & Communication Engineer.

 

He is well known for openly critisicing Tech fails and providing useful information to the public.
